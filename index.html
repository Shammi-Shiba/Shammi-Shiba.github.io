    CoroPy: A Deep Learning Based Comparison Between X-Ray and CT Scan Images in Covid-19 Detection and Classification
    Author: Rahman M.J., Niloy, A.H., Shiba, S.A., Fahim, S.M.F.A., Faria, F.N., Hussain, E., Parvez, M.D. (2021)
    Coronavirus 2019 (in short, Covid-19), was first found in the Wuhan province of China in December 2019 and has been declared a global pandemic by WHO in March 2020.So, we performed a comparative analysis between x-ray and CT san images. Therefore, has insisted us to perform a comparative analysis between X-ray and CT scan images. Thus we came up with a novel CNN model named CoroPy which works for both the image types and can detect normal, Covid-19 and viral pneumonia with great accuracy and shows that X-ray images have overall better performance.
